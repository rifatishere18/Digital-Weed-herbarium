Anguli Ghash

Local name: আঙ্গুলি ঘাস, বড় কাঁকড়া , কাঁকড়া আঙুল ঘাস

English name: Hairy crabgrass, Hairy finger-grass, Large crabgrass, Crab finger grass, Purple crabgrass.

Scientific name: Digitaria sanguinalis

Family: Poaceae

Habit

Annual grass, herb.

Habitat

Dry to moist soils. Cannot persist in water logged area.

Growing season

Kharif.

Associated crops

Aus rice, maize, tea and cotton etc.

Propagating organ

Only by seeds; prolific seed producer. Short seed dormancy.

Leaf

Lamina and leaf cover hairy, simple.

Stem

Erect or creeping, dense branching.

Inflorescence

Spike looks like finger.

Root

Extensive root system gives it an advantage for below ground competition.

Control measures

Deep ploughing, narrow leaf selective herbicide.
